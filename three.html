The Process

1

Registration

In order to donate your organs, corneas, tissues, hands, face, blood, bone marrow, or stem cells, you must register before the time of your death. If your passing occurs in a hospital, you must be kept on a ventilator to ensure the health and life of the organs

3

Matching Process

The Organ Procurement and Transplantation Network is contacted with notice of a possible donation

2

Brain Death Testing

When you pass away, testing is done to ensure that there is no brain activity. Transplantation is never done if there is any question of the patient still showing brain activity.

4

Transplantation

Surgery is performed to remove what is to be transplanted. All incisions are closed, and the body of the donor is treated with the same care and compassion than any living patient would be given.
