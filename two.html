Why Register

to donate?

In the U.S., there are 114,000 people waiting for a transplant.

20 people die each day waiting for a transplant.

Only 3 in 1,000 people die in a way that makes donation possible. (This is why registration is necessary!)

In the United States, death is a taboo subject. The truth is that we all will die, but when we die, more than just our memory can live on. Motheres, fathers, children, brothers, sisters, and grandparents can get a second chance at life.
Donor recipients live each day knowing that their life is only possible because of the sacrifice you made.
